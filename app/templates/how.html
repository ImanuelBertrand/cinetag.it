How?

Start with Browse. Every movie can be tagged with 👍️, 🤷 and 👎.

  • 👍️ You want to see this movie (movies will be marked with a green border)
  • 🤷 You might want to see this movie (blue border)
  • 👎️ You don't want to see this movie (red border)

Once you've tagged a few movies with 👍️, you can check out your releases, also available as a calendar.

Feel free to find your way around and discover things, like your personal lists, e-mail (and other) notifications, etc.

Please note that for technical reasons, some features (like notifications) are only available after creating a user account.
